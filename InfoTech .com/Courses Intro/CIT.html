CIT:What is it about?

CIT is a Certification In Information Technology.

The program begins with an introduction to Information Technology, covering the basics of computer hardware and networking. It also includes comprehensive training in Office Automation, covering MS Office applications such as MS Word, MS Excel, MS PowerPoint, and Internet usage. Additionally, the program offers training in graphical orientation using Adobe Photoshop and provides a solid understanding of web designing through Adobe Dreamweaver and visual studio code.
