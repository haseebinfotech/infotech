Infotech Systems, was established in JUNE 2000, by the idea of Muhammad Ayub (Lecturer in Computer Sciences, Govt. Degree College, Taxila). It is the first Private Sector organization in Tehsil Taxila to support all matters related to Information Technology. Since its inception the organization has achieved many a mile-stones advocating standard, excellence and quality recognition.

A leading organization for Computers & Communications in Taxila and its premises :

  • Promoting Information Technology
  • Helping Innovation in Education
  • Information System Development
  • Providing IT solutions
  • Web Development
  • Sale & Services (Branded / New Computers & Accessories)
  • Computer Networking
  • Scanning , Composing & Printing
